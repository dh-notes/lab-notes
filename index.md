<i>Lab Notes</i> publishes short, peer-reviewed papers on research design and method in the
humanities and social sciences. <span class="hidden md:inline"> We document procedures, workflows, and forms of coordination
that organize research in practice, from data collection through annotation, modeling, and
interpretation. These materials are often produced en route to publication or required to
sustain collaborative, lab-based work.</span>
