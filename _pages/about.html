About

Lab Notes publishes concise, peer-reviewed methodological notes (1,200–2,500 words) on the often invisible or informal processes of collective, lab-based research in the humanities and social sciences.

We foreground methodological experimentation, best practices, and meta-reflection on the artifacts typically left out of formal scholarship. Our mission is to make the metaphorical laboratory a concrete site of shared inquiry, grounded in the material conditions of contemporary knowledge production.
